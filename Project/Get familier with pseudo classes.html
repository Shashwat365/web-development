About Space

Space is the three-dimensional expanse containing all matter and events. It encompasses celestial bodies, galaxies, and the universe as a whole. Space exploration involves investigating physical conditions through satellites, telescopes, and other observational techniques. Key concepts include dark matter, exoplanets, and the cosmos.
